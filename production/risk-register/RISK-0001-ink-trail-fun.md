# Risk: Ink Trail Core Loop May Feel Fiddly

## Identification

- **ID**: RISK-0001
- **Identified By**: Codex bootstrap
- **Date Identified**: 2026-03-28
- **Category**: Design

## Assessment

- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: Major
- **Risk Score**: Medium x Major = High

## Description

The game's identity depends on players enjoying the act of creating and riding
their own temporary routes. If drawing a trail feels slower, clumsier, or less
rewarding than simply moving normally, the core fantasy collapses.
